About Us

Our Story

Damascus Projects was founded to help bridge the gap between ambitious entrepreneurs and investors. We believe that by empowering people to own businesses, homes, and global opportunities, we can transform entire communities across Africa.

Our Mission

To build 50 million businesses and create 500 million jobs across Africa.

Our Vision

To see every willing person own a sustainable business, a comfortable home, and global access.

Our Promise

Vetted projects, transparent processes, and long-term support.
